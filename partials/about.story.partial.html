*Our Story

EchoWave Radio is your favorite wave in New York. Thousands of citizens listen to our broadcasts, playlists, and podcasts while they commute, work, cook, love, and simply live.

At EchoWave, we believe in the extraordinary power of music to transcend boundaries, evoke emotions, and create lasting connections. Our journey began in New York in 2005, when our owner, Jack Richardson, met other music enthusiasts with a shared vision: to redefine the auditory experience.

Today, EchoWave stands as a beacon of musical diversity, a community united by the love for extraordinary sounds. Reflect with us on our history, filled with gratitude for each note, each beat, and each listener who has been a part of this incredible journey. Here's to the years gone by and the countless beats yet to be discovered. Thank you for being part of our musical evolution.

And as we look ahead, we remain committed to pushing the boundaries of sound, innovation, and artistic expression. At EchoWave, we embrace every genre, every rhythm, and every voice that contributes to the rich tapestry of music. Our passion drives us to discover new talent, refine our craft, and bring immersive experiences to listeners around the world.

From the intimate strum of an acoustic guitar to the electrifying pulse of electronic beats, we celebrate the universal language of music in all its forms. Together, we create moments that resonate beyond time—melodies that inspire, rhythms that move, and harmonies that unite.

The journey is far from over. With every new track, every shared experience, and every connection forged through music, we continue to write the next chapter of EchoWave. Here’s to the soundscapes of tomorrow and the endless possibilities that lie ahead. Let’s keep the music alive—together.
